How to Plan a Kid-Friendly Beach Cleanup

To ensure the event is both safe and impactful, consider these steps:

Essential Supplies: Provide buckets or trash bags, gloves to protect hands from sharp objects, and litter pickers if available.

Safety First: Always start with a safety briefing. Use sun protection like hats and sunscreen, and ensure children stay within a designated survey area.

Make it Educational: Before starting, discuss where rubbish comes from and its effect on the ocean. You can even weigh the collected trash at the end to show the kids the tangible difference they made.

Join an Event: Look for local organizations like the Marine Conservation Society or participate in global initiatives like International Coastal Cleanup Day.
